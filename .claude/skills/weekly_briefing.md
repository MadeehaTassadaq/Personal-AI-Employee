# SKILL: Weekly CEO Briefing

## Purpose
Generate executive summary of the week's activity.

## Inputs
- `period`: Date range (default: last 7 days)

## Data Sources
- vault/Dashboard.md
- vault/Done/ (completed tasks)
- vault/Needs_Action/ (pending tasks)
- vault/Logs/ (activity logs)

## Output Format
```markdown
# Weekly CEO Briefing - {{DATE_RANGE}}

## Executive Summary
{{2-3 sentence overview}}

## Completed This Week
- Task 1 (completed DATE)
- Task 2 (completed DATE)

## In Progress
- Task 3 (started DATE, status)

## Pending Approval
- Action 1 (waiting since DATE)

## Bottlenecks
- {{Any blocked or delayed items}}

## Recommendations
- {{Proactive suggestions}}

---
*Generated by Digital FTE on {{GENERATION_DATE}}*
```

## Rules
- Factual only - never fabricate metrics
- Highlight delays honestly
- Keep recommendations actionable
- Tone: Professional, concise, CEO-appropriate
- Save to vault/Plans/weekly-briefing-{{DATE}}.md

## Example Output
```markdown
# Weekly CEO Briefing - Jan 15-22, 2026

## Executive Summary
This week saw 8 tasks completed with 2 pending approval.
Email response times improved by processing client inquiries within 24 hours.

## Completed This Week
- Client onboarding email sent (Jan 18)
- Invoice reminder drafted (Jan 19)
- LinkedIn post published (Jan 20)

## In Progress
- Quarterly report preparation (started Jan 21)

## Pending Approval
- Partnership proposal email (waiting since Jan 21)

## Recommendations
- Review pending partnership email - time-sensitive
- Consider scheduling social posts in advance
```
